ABOUT US

This is one of the country's most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading Bangladeshi restaurant group. Fink and Executive Chef Peter Gilmore. The restaurant is an organic space reflection of Peter Gilmore's nature inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant's place in the dress circle of Sydney Harbour. An ode to the landscape, from the vast ocean floor to the cracked bark of a paperbark tree, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered.
